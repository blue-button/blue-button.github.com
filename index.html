Blue Button

The Blue Button is the symbol for the way that you can access your digital health records. Just click on it and securely download your medical records from your health insurer, provider, or other source of your health data.

Your Data

You can download the file of your personal data and save the file on your own personal computer. After you have saved it, you can import that same file into other computer-based personal health management tools.

Downloading your health data gives you control over your health information. It makes it easy to share with your doctors, caregivers, or anyone else you choose.

Safe & Secure

The Blue Button is safe, secure, reliable, and easy to use. At the same time, since you control access to your downloaded health information, it's your responsibility to protect it. You should treat your personal and health information just like you would your banking or other confidential information.
